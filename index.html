Croquetas Brasileñas, or Salgadinho, is a popular Brazilian snack that is typically served at parties and events. These small savory bites can be made with a variety of fillings, including meat, cheese, and vegetables, and are often deep-fried or baked until crispy. Salgadinhos are a delicious and convenient finger food that can be enjoyed as a quick snack or as part of a larger meal.
